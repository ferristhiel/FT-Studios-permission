FAQ

Can I fork the repo?

Yes, for personal learning or private use with visible credit. Public redistribution or publishing modified versions requires written permission.

Can I upload the game elsewhere?

No, do not re‑host builds or source on marketplaces/CDNs without permission. Always link to the official page instead.

Can I monetize videos?

Yes, you may record/stream gameplay and monetize videos as long as you credit FT‑Studios clearly in the description.
